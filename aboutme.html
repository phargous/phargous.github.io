About Me

A bit about me

My whole childhood, I have lived close to the sea. For sure it has oriented my choices to be today a PhD student in physical oceanography. I graduated from ENSEEIHT (a french engineering school) as an engineer in fluid mechaninics and obtained a Msc in Ocean, Atmosphere and Climate Sciences, I moved to the Canary Islands where I graduated in Marine Science, and later completed a MSc in Oceanography. Wanting to understand the physical conditions and processes within the ocean, I pursued a Ph.D. in Oceanography and Climate Change, specializing in Physical Oceanography, at the Instituto de Oceanografía y Cambio Global (IOCAG) of the University of Las Palmas de Gran Canaria (ULPGC), which I completed in April 2025. After this journey, I am back home in Barcelona, continuing to follow the passion that shaped my career, studying the ocean and its role in climate change as a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Barcelona Supercomputing Center (BSC).
